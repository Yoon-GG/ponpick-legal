PonPick Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 13, 2025

Privacy Officer: ponpick

Contact: ponpickkonpick@gmail.com

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it, including:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol address (IP address)
  • Pages visited within the Application, including time and date
  • Time spent on the Application
  • Operating system of your mobile device

The Application does not collect precise location data.

For an enhanced experience, the Service Provider may require you to provide certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:

  • Email address
  • Nickname
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Device ID
  • Uploaded photos (product scans)
  • Review content
  • App/device information (OS version, app version)
  • Usage logs, error reports, crash data

This information will be retained and used as described in this privacy policy.

Collection and Use Purposes

Collected information is used for:

  • Service provision and user management
  • Product recognition, search, and recommendations
  • Community operation (displaying reviews and photos to other users)
  • Service quality improvement, error analysis, and fraud prevention
  • Displaying user-uploaded photos when external sources (e.g., Rakuten API) lack product images

User-uploaded photos may be displayed to other users within the app.

Third-Party Services

The Application utilizes the following third-party services to improve functionality:

  • Google Firebase (authentication, database, analytics, crashlytics)
  • Google ML Kit (OCR text recognition)
  • Google Cloud Vision API (OCR text recognition)
  • Naver Papago API (translation)
  • Rakuten API (product information)
  • Apple Sign In (authentication on iOS)
  • Google Play Services
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics

Only aggregated, anonymized data is transmitted to these services. The Service Provider does not sell or share your information with third parties for purposes other than service provision.

Some data may be stored or processed on servers located outside your country (e.g., Firebase).

Please refer to the privacy policies of these third-party services:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase

The Service Provider may disclose information:

  • As required by law (e.g., subpoena or legal process)
  • To protect rights, safety, or prevent fraud
  • To trusted service providers under confidentiality agreements

User-Generated Content (UGC)

  • Users retain copyright ownership of uploaded photos and reviews.
  • By uploading content, users grant PonPick a non-exclusive, royalty-free, worldwide license to store, host, display, and modify (e.g., thumbnail, resize) the content for service provision and quality improvement.
  • Content that infringes on third-party rights or is illegal/harmful may be removed without prior notice.
  • Uploaded product photos may be publicly displayed to other users.

Data Retention and Deletion

  • User-provided data will be retained as long as you use the Application and for a reasonable period thereafter, or as required by law.
  • Upon account deletion, related personal information and user-uploaded reviews/photos will be deleted (except where legal retention is required).
  • Public posts (reviews/photos) may be deleted or made private.

To request data deletion, contact us at ponpickkonpick@gmail.com or use the in-app "Delete Account" feature.

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, delete, or suspend processing of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Exercise these rights via the in-app "Delete Account" feature or by emailing ponpickkonpick@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

The Service Provider does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 14.

If we discover that a child under 14 has provided personal information, we will immediately delete it. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at ponpickkonpick@gmail.com.

Security Measures

The Service Provider employs reasonable security measures, including:

  • Access control and authentication
  • Encryption (data transmission protection)
  • Backup and log management

Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all data collection by uninstalling the Application using standard uninstall processes available on your mobile device or app marketplace.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The Service Provider will notify you of significant changes via in-app notice or email at least 7 days in advance.

Continued use of the Application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Consent

By using the Application, you consent to the processing of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.
